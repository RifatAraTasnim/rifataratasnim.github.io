MoMVR

Overview

MoMVR is a Virtual Reality adaptation of Militant of the Maze (MoM), developed for the Meta Quest headset. This project translates the smartphone-based serious game into an immersive VR environment to investigate how the shift in platform and the increased sense of presence influence player experience and therapeutic effectiveness in the treatment procedures for Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD).

Key Highlights

  • Developed a complete VR version of the MoM game for the Meta Quest headset
  • Conducted user studies with over 60 participants and performed statistical analysis to evaluate player experience and therapeutic effectiveness in VR settings
