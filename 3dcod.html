3DCoD & 3DCoDB

Overview

3DCoD and 3DCoDB are 3D adaptations of the ARCoD platform. This project investigates how environmental context and interaction design influence player responses and experience in identifying cognitive distortions.

Key Highlights

  • Developed a 3D version of the ARCoD platform with two input modalities: button-based (3DCoDB) and gesture-based (3DCoD) interaction
  • Conducted experiments on over 60 participants and performed the required statistical analysis
  • Investigated the impact of environmental context on player responses and experience
